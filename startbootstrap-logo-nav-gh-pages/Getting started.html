Getting Started

The first thing you want to know about a PC is what the parts do and how they work. This page will explain most of that simplefied

The CPU

The CPU is the part of the computer that controls everthing it is bassicly the brain of the computer.

The CPU takes inputs from the computer’s RAM and processes the action, before delivering an output.

CPUs are in all sorts of devices ranging from computers and laptops, to smartphones, and tablets.

The chip is placed onto the device’s motherboard and interacts with the other hardware to operate your computer.

The GPU

The GPU also known as the grapichs processing unit is a specialized chip made to accelerate the creation of an image.

It is a single-chip processor that creates effects and transforms objects every time a 3D scene is redrawn.

You can have a dedicated GPU for your computer but some have them integrated in the CPU.

The GPU is mainly used in applications like 3D modeling and gaming.
